Sinigang na Pork Recipe

Description

  • 1 kg pork belly or ribs, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 radish, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach)
  • 100g tamarind paste or sinigang mix
  • 2-3 green chili peppers
  • Fish sauce to taste

Steps

  1. Boil pork with onion and tomatoes in water for 45 minutes or until tender.
  2. Add radish and cook until slightly tender.
  3. Add eggplant and green chili peppers. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in tamarind paste or sinigang mix. Adjust sourness as needed.
  5. Add kangkong and fish sauce to taste. Simmer for another 2 minutes. Serve hot.
