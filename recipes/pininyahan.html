Pininyahan Recipe

Description

A sweet and savory Filipino dish where chicken is cooked with pineapples and vegetables in a creamy coconut sauce.

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 can (200g) pineapple chunks with juice
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and onion until soft.
  2. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in pineapple chunks with juice and fish sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add coconut milk, carrots, and bell pepper. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
