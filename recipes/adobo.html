Adobong Baboy Recipe

Description

A classic Filipino dish made with pork braised in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices. It's savory, slightly tangy, and aromatic.

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate pork in soy sauce and garlic for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pot, then brown the pork.
  3. Add the marinade, vinegar, bay leaves, peppercorns, and water. Do not stir.
  4. Simmer covered for 40-50 minutes until pork is tender.
  5. Uncover and simmer until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper.
